Assembly Hall

Every morning, Omkar Bhoomi Vandana, prayer, Gayatri Mantra, Shanti Mantra, meditation and spiritual practices are conducted. Students participate in morning exercise from 06:00 to 06:40 including running, warm-up, pranayama, yoga, meditation, sadhana and Surya Namaskar.

Sports Activities

Regular practice is conducted for 11 sports including volleyball, football, cricket, kabaddi, kho-kho, basketball, skating, archery, horse riding, table tennis and martial arts. Special attention is given to encourage students to reach state and national levels under expert guidance.

Accommodation

Separate arrangements for each student with open and airy living spaces, personal cupboards, study tables and laundry facilities ensure comfort and discipline.

Bhojan Kaksh / Dining Hall

Students are provided with healthy and balanced meals. Maharashtrian-style lunch, nutritious breakfast, milk and supplements ensure proper growth and well-being.

Library - 24 / 7 Study Room

A peaceful and well-organized library provides students with a rich collection of books and a calm study atmosphere, encouraging strong reading habits and intellectual growth.

Counseling Facility

Professional counseling support is available for students facing academic or personal challenges, ensuring emotional well-being and proper guidance.

Medical Facilities

Regular health check-ups are conducted and necessary medical support is provided. Doctors visit twice a week and healthcare awareness sessions are organized.

Swimming Facility

Swimming guidance and practice sessions are conducted for students of classes 6th to 8th every Sunday and on the second and fourth Saturdays of each month.
