Assembly hall

Every morning, Omkar Bhoomi Vandana, prayer, Gayatri Mantra, Shanti Mantra, meditation, etc. Morning exercise every morning from 06:00 to 06:40 Regular exercise types Running Warm up Pranayama Yoga Meditation Sadhana Surya Namaskar etc. Exercise

Sports Activities

Regular practice is conducted for 11 sports, including volleyball, football, cricket, kabaddi, kho-kho, basketball, skating, archery, horse riding, table tennis, and martial arts and karate for self-defense. Special attention is paid to ensuring that as many players as possible reach the state and national levels. Experienced guides and sports coaches are available for all sports.

Accommodation

Separate island arrangement for each student, open and airy living room, separate cupboard for storing materials, study table for studying, laundry facility for each student.

Bhojan Kaksh / Dinning Hall

Meals are provided to students in a healthy and balanced diet. Maharashtrian style meals are served in the lunch. Breakfast, lunch, milk, almonds.

Library - 24 / 7 Study Room

The school library provides a peaceful and well-organized space where students can read, learn, and explore new ideas. With a rich collection of books and a calm study atmosphere, it encourages every child to develop strong reading habits.

Counseling Facility

If children have any academic or personal difficulties, counseling from expert guidance is available

Medical facilities

If any health problems arise in the daily life of the students, necessary medication will be provided in the Gurukul. Doctors will conduct medical check-ups and health care classes for sick students twice a week.

Swimming Facitity

Swimming guidance and practice for students of classes 6th to 8th every Sunday, second and fourth Saturday of the month
