Late Smt. B.G.Shanbagh Vidyalaya

Imagine a school that goes beyond traditional education—one that nurtures students mentally, physically, and emotionally. This institution is not just a place for academics but a guiding force that shapes young minds with cultural, spiritual, and artistic values.

The curriculum blends academics with thoughtfully chosen co-curricular activities, ensuring overall development. Along with intellectual growth, the school focuses on building character, discipline, and creativity.

A strong emphasis is placed on patriotism and cultural heritage. Throughout the year, students participate in meaningful celebrations, festivals, and programs that help them connect deeply with the nation’s culture and history. These events become vibrant learning experiences where students explore arts, music, and drama while expressing their talents.

In this enriching environment, students grow into well-rounded individuals—rooted in values, confident in abilities, and prepared to serve as responsible citizens. The school becomes a place where knowledge, culture, and humanity come together to shape a brighter future.

Education is meant to make life better, help personal growth, enhance thinking abilities, and lead to good morals.

-Dr. Avinash Ramachandra Acharya

Residential School

Education and living facilities for students up to standard 10, fostering academic and personal growth in a communal setting.

Vivekanand Gurukul (Residential School)

10-acre campus dominated by a majestic, well-built building. This educational sanctuary boasts a vast playground, inviting both recreation and sports. The institution prides itself on offering 24-hour residential teaching, ensuring round-the-clock guidance and support for its students. A dedicated Health Department caters to the well-being of the pupils, while the canteen serves nutritious and delicious meals, emphasizing the importance of a balanced diet.

The students here are actively engaged in social activities, contributing to their holistic development. Beyond conventional academics, the school provides special guidance, fostering qualities like moral education, love, self-dependence, and self-reliance. The ethos of the institution revolves around precise time management and discipline, creating an environment conducive to learning and personal growth.

Accommodating up to 300 students, the boarding and lodging facilities are top-notch, offering a comfortable and nurturing home away from home. Additionally, the campus features specialized coaches and separate playgrounds for a variety of games, ensuring that every student has the opportunity to explore and excel in their chosen sport.
