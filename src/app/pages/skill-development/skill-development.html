Technical Lab

Our school has a separate Technical Lab that provides students with hands-on learning experiences in science and technology. The lab is well-equipped with modern tools, instruments, and resources to support practical knowledge and innovation. Students actively participate in activities such as electronics, robotics, model-making, and basic engineering experiments. Regular workshops and project-based sessions are conducted to enhance technical skills and creativity. The lab encourages curiosity and helps learners apply theoretical concepts in real-life situations. Trained teachers and mentors guide students throughout their projects, ensuring safety and effective learning. Competitions, exhibitions, and innovation challenges are frequently organized to showcase student talent. The lab environment promotes teamwork, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills. Many students develop innovative models and solutions through the activities conducted here. Our Technical Lab stands as a hub of creativity and innovation, preparing students for a technology-driven future.

Mati Kam (Clay Work)

Our school has a separate Mati Kam (clay work) section that provides students with a creative space to explore art and craftsmanship. This area is designed to help learners develop patience, focus, and imagination through hands-on clay modeling. Students engage in a variety of activities such as making pots, sculptures, decorative items, and artistic models. Regular sessions are conducted under the guidance of skilled instructors who encourage creativity and skill-building. Working with clay improves fine motor skills, concentration, and artistic expression among students. Exhibitions and displays are organized to showcase their beautiful creations. The activity also teaches the value of traditional art forms and connects students with cultural heritage. Group projects and workshops held here foster teamwork and collaboration. Many students develop unique talents and discover new interests through Mati Kam. This dedicated space truly reflects our school’s effort to nurture creativity, innovation, and holistic development.

Murti Kam (Sculpture Making)

Creativity finds its true expression in our school’s dedicated Murti Kaam section. This unique space allows students to explore the traditional art of idol and sculpture making. Under the guidance of experienced instructors, learners gain hands-on experience in shaping clay and other materials into beautiful artistic forms. The activity not only enhances imagination but also develops patience, focus, and fine motor skills. Students regularly create idols and models for school exhibitions, cultural events, and festive celebrations. Working in groups helps them share ideas, collaborate, and learn the values of teamwork. The section also connects young minds with our cultural heritage and artistic traditions. Many students discover hidden skills and a passion for art through these sessions. Competitions and displays provide opportunities to showcase their talent and creativity. Our Murti Kaam facility truly enriches the learning environment by blending art, culture, and holistic development.

Computer Activities

The lab is equipped with updated computers, internet facilities, and essential software to support skill development. Students engage in activities such as coding, graphic design, presentations, digital art, and basic programming. Regular workshops and practice sessions are organized to enhance technological knowledge and creativity. Trained instructors guide learners to use computers effectively and responsibly. These activities also help students improve problem-solving, logical thinking, and innovation skills. Participation in digital competitions and projects gives them valuable exposure and confidence. The facility encourages teamwork as students often collaborate on group projects and assignments. Many students develop strong technical interests and pursue new ideas through this platform. Our computer-based activity section truly reflects the school’s commitment to preparing students for a technology-driven future.
