Technical Lab

Our Technical Lab provides hands-on learning experiences in science and technology. Equipped with modern tools, robotics kits, electronics systems and engineering resources, students engage in project-based learning, innovation challenges, and creative experimentation. The lab nurtures curiosity, teamwork, and problem-solving while preparing students for a technology-driven future.

Mati Kam (Clay Work)

The Mati Kam section encourages artistic expression through clay modeling. Students create pots, sculptures, decorative items and artistic models while developing patience, creativity and focus. Guided workshops and exhibitions promote cultural appreciation and craftsmanship.

Murti Kam (Sculpture Making)

Our Murti Kam section allows students to explore sculpture and idol making. Under expert guidance, learners shape artistic forms while enhancing focus, imagination and fine motor skills. The section blends culture, creativity, and holistic development beautifully.

Computer Activities

The Computer Activities section provides modern digital learning opportunities including coding, design, programming and digital innovation. Equipped with updated systems and guided by trained instructors, this section prepares students for the future through technology and creativity.
